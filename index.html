Questions, Answered

Do I need to provide anything?

Just access to water and a power outlet. We bring everything else: all products, tools, and equipment.

How long does a detail take?

Two to three hours, depending on your vehicle's size and condition. We'll give you a time estimate when you book.

Do I need to be home?

Nope. As long as we can access your vehicle, water, and power, you can go about your day. We'll text you when it's done.

What if it rains?

We'll reach out to reschedule at no charge. Weather happens, and we'd rather deliver a perfect detail than a rushed one.

Where do you go?

We serve the Tri-Cities and surrounding areas within a 20 mile radius, including Kennewick, Richland, Pasco, West Richland, Benton City, Finley, and Burbank.

How do I pay?

We accept cash, Venmo, and check after the job is done and you've seen the results.
